Safety

Death and serious injury can occur if improper use of the firearm is undertaken. Please read this manual and it's warnings fully before loading or operating the Behring P8-SG Ballistic Shutgun. Keep this manual with the firearm at all times and ensure it is transfered with it when loaned or sold.

⚠ WARNINGNegligent discharge can occur if the weapon is mishandled causing serious injury, damage, or death.

⚠ WARNINGAlways handle the weapon as if it is loaded (even if advised otherwise) until you can safeuly and accurately determine otherwise.

⚠ WARNINGNever point the muzzle at anything you don't intend to fire upon.

⚠ WARNINGKeep your finger away from the trigger and guard until you are ready to discharge the weapon.

⚠ WARNINGHand over of the weapon should be performed with the muzzle pointed downward, away from targets and ideally fully unloaded.

⚠ WARNINGModification of the firearm should only be performed by a licenced professional as malfunction can occur leading to serious injury and death.

⚠ WARNINGStorage should be in an unloaded state in the supplied casing or appropriate locker.

⚠ WARNINGStorage should be in an unloaded state in the supplied casing or appropriate locker.

⚠ WARNINGChildren and unauthorised persons should be unable to handle the weapon at all times.

⚠ WARNINGPersuent of local laws, it is advised that ammunition be stored separately from the firearm when in storage.
